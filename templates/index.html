Getting Started

Step 1: Download Your Strava Data

1. Go to Strava Settings
2. Click "My Account" in the left sidebar
3. Scroll down to "Download or Delete Your Account"
4. Request your archive and download it when ready

Step 2: Extract Your Data

Extract the downloaded ZIP file to a folder on your computer.
The activities folder will contain your GPX/FIT/TCX files.

Tips:

  • Make sure the path points to the "activities" folder in your Strava export
  • The folder should contain .gpx, .fit, or .tcx files
  • Processing may take a few seconds if you have many activities
  • The heatmap will show all your activities combined
